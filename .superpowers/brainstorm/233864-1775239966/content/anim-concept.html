Animation Direction — Hex Pulse

Three approaches for ambient idle animation. Click a card to select it.

Option A

Synchronized Breathe

All hexes inhale/exhale together at slightly staggered offsets — center first, satellites follow. Slow, organic, like breathing.

Option B

Radial Wave

A pulse starts at center and ripples outward — each satellite expands in sequence by distance from center. Like dropping a stone in water.

Option C

Independent Float

Each hex drifts on its own slow rhythm — slight scale and vertical drift, all at different speeds. Feels like they're alive and independent.

You can also combine: e.g. "B for hexes + keep the existing label pulse". Let me know in terminal.
